Company By Farhan Ahmed, Founder & CEO

SalwaDev is a Pakistan-based web development and AI company helping startups and enterprises in Pakistan and the United Kingdom ship faster, rank higher, and grow with confidence. This is the story of who we are, who leads us, and how we build.

If you are searching for a reliable web development company in Pakistan — or a software house that works with UK clients — you have probably noticed how hard it is to separate marketing noise from real engineering capability. SalwaDev was founded to close exactly that gap: world-class digital execution, founder-level accountability, and pricing that makes sense for businesses in both Pakistani and UK markets.

Who We Are

SalwaDev is a founder-led digital agency headquartered in Rawalpindi, Pakistan, delivering custom web development, AI agents, SEO, and Shopify e-commerce to clients across Pakistan, the UK, and global markets. We are deliberately small and senior — every project is touched by people who have shipped real products, not juniors learning on your budget.

Our work spans enterprise websites, web apps, Shopify stores, AI automation, technical SEO, and brand design. Whether you are a UK e-commerce brand that needs a faster Shopify storefront, a Pakistani SME launching its first professional website, or a startup that needs an AI agent to handle customer support, we build it end to end.

Founder & Leadership Team

SalwaDev is led by three people whose names are on the door — and who still ship work every week. Our leadership combines hands-on engineering with real business strategy, which is rare in the Pakistani software house landscape.

SalwaDev Leadership

Farhan Ahmed Founder & CEO

Farhan Ahmed is the Founder and CEO of SalwaDev. He set the company's vision and continues to lead client strategy and full-stack development. With 3+ years building products for Pakistani and international clients, Farhan founded SalwaDev to prove that a Pakistan-based team can deliver work that competes with any UK or US agency. As both founder and CEO, he stays personally involved in the technical and commercial direction of every major engagement.

Obaid Ullah Co-CEO

Obaid Ullah serves as Co-CEO of SalwaDev, driving growth, operations, and client partnerships. He ensures that the company's promises translate into measurable outcomes — and that every client relationship, in Pakistan or the UK, delivers lasting value.

Zain Ul Abideen CTO

Zain Ul Abideen is the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) of SalwaDev. He owns the company's engineering direction and technical quality across React, Node.js, WordPress, and AI systems, making sure everything we ship is fast, secure, and built to scale.

Together, Founder & CEO Farhan Ahmed, Co-CEO Obaid Ullah, and CTO Zain Ul Abideen form a leadership team that is technical, accountable, and genuinely invested in client success.

Why Pakistan + UK Is a Winning Combination

Pakistan has become one of the world's strongest hubs for software development, IT outsourcing, and digital services. The country produces tens of thousands of skilled developers each year, and the time-zone overlap with the UK makes real-time collaboration easy. For UK businesses, partnering with a quality Pakistani software house means access to senior engineering talent at a fraction of London agency rates — without sacrificing quality or communication.

SalwaDev was built for exactly this bridge. We understand UK client expectations around quality, GDPR-aware data handling, and professional communication, while operating from a Pakistani cost base that lets ambitious companies do more with their budget.

  • For UK clients: senior developers, fast delivery, transparent fixed pricing, and a time zone that overlaps your working day.
  • For Pakistani clients: enterprise-grade websites and AI systems that meet international standards, built locally in Rawalpindi.
  • For startups everywhere: a founder-led team that moves fast — from brief to working prototype in 48 hours.

What We Build

Web Development (Pakistan & UK)

We build high-performance websites and web apps using React, Next.js, Node.js, and WordPress. From landing pages to full-stack platforms, our web development services are engineered for speed, SEO, and conversion — whether the audience is in Karachi, Lahore, London, or Manchester.

AI Agents & Automation

Our AI agents handle customer support, lead generation, content workflows, and repetitive business processes around the clock. AI is one of the fastest-growing areas in both the Pakistani and UK tech markets, and we help businesses adopt it without the hype.

SEO Services

Technical SEO, on-page optimisation, and content strategy that compounds over time. Our SEO services help Pakistani and UK businesses rank for the keywords that actually drive revenue — not vanity traffic.

Shopify & E-Commerce

Custom Shopify storefronts, checkout optimisation, and app integrations. Shopify development is in high demand among UK retailers, and we build stores that convert browsers into buyers.

How We Work

We keep it refreshingly simple: a discovery call, a clear fixed-price proposal within 48 hours, weekly progress via WhatsApp, and 30 days of free post-launch support. No account managers, no telephone game — you talk directly to the people building your product, including, on key projects, the founder himself.
