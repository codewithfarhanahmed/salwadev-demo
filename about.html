Our Story

Born in Pakistan.
Built for the World.

SalwaDev was founded in Rawalpindi with a clear mandate: bring world-class digital execution to businesses that deserve better. We saw too many companies settle for mediocre websites and templated solutions that didn't reflect their ambitions.

Three years and 50+ projects later, we're Pakistan's most results-focused digital agency — serving clients from local SMEs to global brands, with the same senior-level attention on every engagement.

We hire people obsessed with craft, we measure success by your metrics, and we build relationships that last beyond the launch date.

Founded and led by Farhan Ahmed (Founder & CEO), alongside Obaid Ullah (Co-CEO) and Zain Ul Abideen (CTO), SalwaDev pairs hands-on engineering leadership with a relentless focus on client outcomes.

Our Values

The Principles That
Drive Our Work

Every decision we make is guided by these core commitments — to our clients, to our craft, and to each other.

01

Results Over Vanity

We build for business outcomes — traffic, conversions, revenue — not just awards and aesthetics. Every design decision is backed by data and strategy.

02

Radical Transparency

You'll always know where your project stands. Weekly updates, honest timelines, and no surprises at invoice time. Clarity is a service we provide.

03

Senior-Level Execution

No juniors learning on your budget. Every project gets the attention of experienced engineers, designers, and strategists who've done this before.

04

Long-Term Partnership

We're not a build-and-disappear agency. Post-launch support, growth optimisation, and ongoing iteration are part of every engagement.

05

Speed Without Shortcuts

We move fast, but we don't cut corners. Our structured delivery process lets us ship quality work in record time — consistently.

06

Craft & Obsession

We genuinely care about how things look and feel. Good enough isn't in our vocabulary. We keep iterating until it's right.
